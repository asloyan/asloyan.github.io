About Me

I'm Aidan, an Economics Masters student at the University of Mannheim in Germany. Originally from Ireland, I completed my Bachelor's degree at University College Dublin. My research interests in economics lie in the areas of public economics, health economics, policy analysis, and applied econometrics. I'm passionate about understanding how economic policies can be used to tackle some of the most pressing social and economic challenges facing society today. In my free time, you'll find me running and playing badminton!
